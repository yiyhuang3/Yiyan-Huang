Causal & Data Science Lab (Causal-Stat Lab)
因果与数据科学实验室（Causal-Stat 实验室）

The Causal & Data Science Lab studies the two-way synergy between causal inference and quantitative data sciences. We develop theory and methods where causal thinking improves ML/optimization, and scalable statistical & ML tools strengthen causal analysis. Applications span domain adaptation, OOD detection, uncertainty & robustness, digital business & operations, environmental / sustainability studies, and bioinformatics.

Research directions
  • A — Causal inference: identification under interference/hidden confounding, counterfactual & causal representation learning, robustness.
  • B — Statistics / ML / OR: scalable estimation, generalization, uncertainty quantification, domain adaptation, constrained decision-making.
  • Synergies & Applications: (a) A→B: causal priors for ML; (b) B→A: scalable/robust estimators; (c) applications in ML, management science, social & biomedical sciences.

Lab & Research
实验室 & 研究方向

Our lab bridges two complementary pillars:

  • A — Causal inference: identification, robustness under violations (interference, hidden confounding, overlap failures), counterfactual reasoning, and causal representation learning.
  • B — Statistics / Data Science / ML / OR: scalable estimation, generalization, model uncertainty, domain adaptation, optimization and decision-making under constraints.

We pursue three kinds of synergy between A and B:

  1. Use A to improve B: integrate causal structure to make ML models more robust and reliable.
  2. Use B to improve A: bring scalable, flexible statistical and ML tools to enhance causal identification and estimation.
  3. Joint applications: apply causal+statistical tools to Machine Learning (domain adaptation, OOD detection, uncertainty and robustness), Management Science (information systems, digital business, operations), Social Science (environment & sustainability) and Biomedical Science (bioinformatics).

If you are interested in theory, method or interdisciplinary applications — or seeking collaboration or student positions — please reach out.

我们的实验室连接两大互补支柱：

  • A — 因果推断：可辨识性、在假设违背下的鲁棒性（干扰、未观测混淆、重叠性失败）、反事实推理与因果表示学习。
  • B — 统计/数据科学/机器学习/运筹学：可扩展估计、泛化与不确定性、域适应、优化与约束下的决策方法。

我们追求A与B之间的三类协同：

  1. 用A提升B：把因果结构融入ML，使模型更稳健、更可靠。
  2. 用B提升A：引入可扩展的统计/机器学习工具，提升因果识别与估计能力。
  3. 结合应用：将因果+统计方法应用到机器学习（域适应、分布外检测、不确定性与鲁棒性）、管理科学（信息系统、数字商业、运筹管理）、社会科学（环境与可持续性）与生物医学（生物信息学）等领域。

欢迎对理论/方法/跨学科应用感兴趣的合作者与学生联系加入。

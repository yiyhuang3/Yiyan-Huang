Welcome to my homepage! I am a Postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Applied Mathematics at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), host by Prof. Min Dai. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Data Science from the School of Data Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), supervised by Prof. Qi Wu. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Finance from the Department of Statistics and Finance at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

I was a visiting research scholar at the Department of Economics, Brown University, hosted by Prof. Toru Kitagawa. Welcome to contact me by email if there is any job opportunity!

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of economics & finance (and fintech) and machine learning, with a special focus on causal inference. I am also working on topics such as unsupervised domain adaptation, credit risk analysis, financial time-sires data science, etc.

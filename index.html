In 2023, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Data Science from the School of Data Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), supervised by Prof. Qi Wu. In 2023, I was also a visiting research scholar at the Department of Economics, Brown University, hosted by Prof. Toru Kitagawa. In 2019, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Finance from the Department of Statistics and Finance at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

    Research Interests

    My research interests lie at the intersections of several disciplines, including Data Science, Causal Inference, Machine Learning, and Data-Driven Decision Making. In particular, most of my work uses machine learning tools to estimate treatment effects in various fields and assist policy-makers in personalized decision making.
